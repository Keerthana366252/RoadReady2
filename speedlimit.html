🚗 Speed Limits

Speed limits help maintain road safety and reduce accidents.

Why Speed Limits Matter

  • Prevent accidents.
  • Allow drivers more reaction time.
  • Protect pedestrians and cyclists.

General Speed Limit Guidelines

  • City Roads – 40–60 km/h
  • Highways – 80–100 km/h
  • School Zones – 20–30 km/h
