🚶 Pedestrian Rules

Pedestrians must follow safety rules to avoid accidents.

Safe Crossing Rules

  • Use zebra crossings whenever possible.
  • Look both ways before crossing.
  • Obey pedestrian traffic signals.
  • Avoid using phones while crossing.

Important Safety Tips

  • Walk on sidewalks where available.
  • Wear visible clothing at night.
  • Be alert near intersections.
