🚦 Traffic Signals

Traffic lights control the flow of vehicles and pedestrians.

🔴 Red Light

Stop your vehicle completely before the stop line.

🟡 Yellow Light

Prepare to stop. Do not speed up to cross the signal.

🟢 Green Light

You may proceed if the road ahead is clear.

Safety Tips

  • Never jump a red light.
  • Always watch pedestrians crossing.
  • Follow signals even if roads look empty.
