🏍 Helmet Safety

Helmets protect riders from serious head injuries during accidents.

Why Helmets Are Important

  • Reduce risk of head injury.
  • Protect the skull and brain.
  • Required by traffic law.

Helmet Safety Tips

  • Always wear a helmet while riding a bike.
  • Ensure the helmet strap is tight.
  • Use certified helmets only.
  • Both rider and pillion must wear helmets.
