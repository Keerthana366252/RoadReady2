🛑 Road Signs

Road signs provide important information and warnings to drivers.

Types of Road Signs

  • Warning Signs – Alert drivers about possible hazards ahead.
  • Mandatory Signs – Indicate rules that must be followed.
  • Information Signs – Provide helpful road information.

Common Signs

  • Stop Sign – Stop completely before proceeding.
  • No Entry – Vehicles are not allowed to enter.
  • Speed Limit – Maximum allowed speed.
  • School Zone – Drive slowly and watch for children.
